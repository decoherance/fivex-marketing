# FiveX Strong Marketing Site

## What This Is

A single-page marketing website for FiveX Strong, a free, local-first workout tracker app available on Google Play. The site drives visitors to download the app by showcasing its key features — pre-defined workout programmes, custom programme creation, and full history tracking — while emphasizing its no-account, no-paywall, privacy-first positioning.

## Core Value

Clearly communicate what FiveX Strong does and get visitors to download the app from Google Play.

## Context

- FiveX Strong is a workout tracker app on Google Play
- Local-first: all data stays on the user's device, no online account required
- Completely free with no features locked behind a paywall
- An existing design prototype exists (dark theme, neon green, bold typography) that serves as the reference design
- Tone should be approachable and confident, not aggressively "elite athlete" — match the accessible, free positioning
- The app name is "FiveX Strong" (not "FiveX Stronger")

### Brand Reference

- **Primary color:** #00E676 (neon green)

- **Headlines:** Space Grotesk (bold/black weight)
- **Body text:** Inter
- **Style:** Dark theme, moderate roundedness, bold uppercase accents

## Constraints

- **Hosting:** GitHub Pages — must output fully static HTML/CSS/JS
- **Framework:** Astro for static site generation
- **Images:** Placeholder slots for app screenshots (not available yet)
- **Single page:** One-shot marketing site, no routing needed
- **Performance:** Fast load times, minimal JS
